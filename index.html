About Me


An engineer turned consultant bringing engineering methodologies and technologies to the business world. As a technology and strategy consultant, I have combined my engineering background with business acumen to lead the digital transformation of risk management processes for some of the world's most well renowned companies in the technology, media and entertainment, healthcare, pharmaceutical and private education sectors.

I am a dedicated problem solver who is always looking for the next challenge to conquer. With an academic background in Industrial and Operations Engineering and a relentless drive to continuously improve, I am always seeking to optimize business operations and generate efficiencies through process improvement and the implementation of new technologies and strategies.

As a passionate advocate for universal education, I not only spend my time volunteering as a teacher and help fundraise for educational initiatives, but by understanding the importance of education, I too am consistently looking to learn, develop, and improve as an individual. I am always searching for new challenges that will help me grow, so reach out if you have an opportunity!
